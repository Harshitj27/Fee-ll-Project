About Explorica

Welcome to Explorica, your gateway to discovering the hidden gems and delicious foods of India's most vibrant cities. Our mission is to showcase the lesser-known spots and unique culinary delights that make each city special.

At Explorica, we believe that every city has a story to tell, and we're here to share those stories with you. Whether you're a seasoned traveller or a curious explorer, our platform will guide you through the hidden treasures of 20 remarkable Indian cities.

Join us on this journey and uncover the secrets of India, one city at a time.
