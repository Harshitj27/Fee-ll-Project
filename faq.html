Frequently Asked Questions

What is Explorica?

Explorica is a platform dedicated to showcasing the hidden spots and unique food items remarkable cities in India. We aim to provide travellers and locals with an inside look at the lesser-known gems of these cities.

How can I explore the cities on Explorica?

You can visit the "Cities" page on our website to explore the cities we feature. Each city has its own dedicated page with information about its hidden spots and must-try food items.

Yes,If you know of a hidden spot or unique food item in one of our featured cities, feel free to contact us through the "Contact Us" page.
