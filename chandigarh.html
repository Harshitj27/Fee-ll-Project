Chandigarh

Explore the top tourist spots and taste the famous foods of Chandigarh

Top Tourist Places in Chandigarh

Garden of Silence

The Garden of Silence in Chandigarh is a peaceful meditation space near Sukhna Lake, featuring a serene Buddha statue. Surrounded by greenery, it offers a quiet retreat for introspection, mindfulness, and connecting with nature in tranquil surroundings.

Capitol Complex

The Capitol Complex in Chandigarh is a UNESCO World Heritage site, designed by architect Le Corbusier. It houses the Punjab and Haryana High Court, Secretariat, and Legislative Assembly, showcasing modernist architecture. The complex symbolizes Chandigarh's visionary urban planning and architectural excellence.

Sukhna Lake

Sukhna Lake in Chandigarh is a man-made reservoir at the foothills of the Shivalik range. It's a popular spot for boating, walking, and picnicking, offering scenic views and serene surroundings. The lake is a hub for recreation and relaxation in the city.



Morni Hills

Morni Hills, located near Chandigarh, is a picturesque hill station in the Shivalik range. Known for its lush greenery, serene lakes, and trekking trails, it offers a peaceful escape and stunning views. It's a favorite destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Pinjore Garden

Pinjore Garden, near Chandigarh, is a historic Mughal garden known for its terraced layout, fountains, and lush greenery. Also called Yadavindra Gardens, it features grand pavilions and a central waterway, making it a popular spot for picnics and leisurely strolls.

Chattbir Zoo

Chattbir Zoo, located near Chandigarh, is a vast zoological park home to a wide variety of animals, including the famous Royal Bengal tiger. Spread over a large area, it offers safari tours and well-maintained enclosures, making it a popular destination for families and wildlife enthusiasts.

Famous Foods of Chandigarh

Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a beloved dish in Chandigarh, often enjoyed as a hearty breakfast or lunch. The spicy chickpea curry, paired with fluffy, deep-fried bhature, is a staple at local eateries. Known for its rich flavors and satisfying portions, it's a must-try for anyone visiting the city.

Aloo Tikki Chaat

Aloo Tikki Chaat is a popular street food in Chandigarh, offering a delicious combination of crispy potato patties topped with tangy chutneys, yogurt, and spices. This flavorful snack is a favorite among locals and visitors, often enjoyed at bustling food stalls and markets across the city.

Kulfi Falooda

Kulfi Falooda is a favorite dessert in Chandigarh, blending rich, creamy kulfi with rose-flavored falooda noodles. Often garnished with nuts and fruit, this refreshing treat is a popular choice for cooling off and satisfying sweet cravings in the city's vibrant food scene.



Lassi

Lassi is a traditional Punjabi drink widely enjoyed in Chandigarh. This refreshing yogurt-based beverage, often flavored with spices or fruit, is popular for its cooling properties and tangy taste. It’s a favorite accompaniment to meals or a standalone treat.

Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is a beloved street food in Chandigarh, featuring a spicy, flavorful vegetable mash served with buttered, toasted bread rolls (pav). Known for its rich taste and satisfying combination, it’s a popular choice at local food stalls and eateries.

Frankie

Frankie is a popular street food in Chandigarh, consisting of a rolled flatbread filled with spiced vegetables or meat, and various chutneys. This versatile and convenient snack is favored for its quick preparation and customizable fillings, making it a local favorite.
